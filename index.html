About Me

Sundhar Karunanidi Web Developer

C#, ASP.Net, JS, Python, CPP, CSS and HTML

Hello and thank you for taking time to read my portfolio. I am a Backend Engineer with lots of experience in C#, ASP.Net (Framework and Core), JavaScript, Python and CPP. Currently I am moving to London and looking at my career role. The majority of work I have done has been for large scale enterprise CI/CD projects for clients such as Greene King, APM, Bet365 and Electrolux as well as numerous goverment organisations. I have also worked in TDD/BDD projects. Recently I have been involved with the Node.js initiative as a mentee to further Node.js. I have been working with Azure and AWS extensively, with a push to make coding more sustainable with the advent of AI. In my current role I have conducted research into the impact of etensive lambda use and how we can track the VMs behind the scenes and the carbon equivalence of them.
